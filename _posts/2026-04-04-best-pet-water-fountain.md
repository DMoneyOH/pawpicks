## What Could Be Better

While the [PetSafe Drinkwell Platinum Pet Fountain 168oz](https://amzn.to/41dtOOM) offers a lot to love, there are a few areas where we think it could be improved, particularly for a product designed for long-term use.

Our primary concern revolves around the **nuances of cleaning, specifically the pump and hard-to-reach areas.** While the main plastic parts are dishwasher safe, the small submersible pump requires manual cleaning. This isn't a huge deal, but the pump itself has tiny nooks and crannies that can accumulate slime and debris over time. If not cleaned meticulously and regularly – we’re talking weekly with a small brush – the pump's efficiency can decrease, and it can become a breeding ground for bacteria. This manual, detailed cleaning requirement for the pump can be a bit tedious and is easy to overlook for busy owners, potentially compromising the overall hygiene of the pet water fountain.

Secondly, **the ongoing cost and need for filter replacements** can add up. The [PetSafe Drinkwell Platinum Pet Fountain 168oz](https://amzn.to/41dtOOM) uses both activated carbon and foam filters, which PetSafe recommends replacing every 2-4 weeks. While these filters are crucial for keeping the water clean and fresh, purchasing them regularly creates a recurring expense. For budget-focused pet owners, this continuous cost for consumables can be a drawback, especially compared to a simple water bowl that requires no such ongoing investment. It's a necessary evil for filtration, but it's something to factor into the total cost of ownership.

Lastly, while the BPA-free plastic construction is generally durable, **some owners may prefer a more robust or hygienic material like stainless steel.** Over time, plastic can scratch, and micro-scratches can become harder to clean, potentially harboring bacteria. While we didn't encounter significant issues during our testing period, the long-term durability and resistance to wear and tear of plastic might not match that of stainless steel, which some pet owners prefer for its perceived superior hygiene and longevity. For a product meant to provide fresh water daily, a more premium material might offer greater peace of mind for some.
