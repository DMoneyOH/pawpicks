## What Could Be Better

While the [PETLIBRO Automatic Cat Feeder 5G WiFi 5L](https://amzn.to/4c4heGp) offers a lot to love, there are a couple of areas where we feel it could be improved or where potential buyers might want to manage their expectations.

### No Integrated Camera for Monitoring

One feature that some premium automatic cat feeder models offer, but which is absent here, is an integrated camera. For pet parents who travel frequently or simply want to check in on their cat during the day, a camera offers visual confirmation that their pet is eating and doing well. While the app provides feeding logs, it doesn't give you that direct visual connection. If seeing your cat eat is a must-have for your peace of mind, you'd need to invest in a separate pet camera alongside this feeder.

### Reliance on the App for Schedule Adjustments

While the app is fantastic for setting up and managing schedules, it's worth noting that all programming and adjustments must be done via the app. There are no manual buttons on the unit itself to set meal times or dispense food, beyond a manual feed button which dispenses one portion. If your WiFi network goes down for an extended period, or if you're in an area without cell service, you won't be able to alter the existing schedule or program new ones until connectivity is restored. The battery backup ensures scheduled meals still dispense, but flexibility is tied to the app.
