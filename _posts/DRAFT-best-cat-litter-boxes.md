## Buying Guide  

Choosing the **best cat litter boxes** isn’t just about size; it’s about matching the box to your cat’s habits and your lifestyle. Here are the key factors we considered.  

**Size and Accessibility** – A box that’s too small forces cats to dig aggressively, which can fling litter everywhere. Measure your cat from nose to tail and add a few inches. Extra‑large models, like the stainless‑steel option, give senior cats room to turn comfortably.  

**Material** – Plastic is lightweight and cheap, but it can absorb odors over time. Stainless steel stays neutral, resists scratches, and is dishwasher‑safe for a deep clean. Some cats enjoy the cool feel of metal, while others may prefer the warmth of plastic.  

**Cover vs. Open** – Covered boxes trap smells but can restrict airflow, leading to a stale environment if not cleaned often. Open trays provide ventilation but may let litter escape. Top‑entry designs strike a middle ground, forcing cats to step in and out through a tunnel that catches stray granules.  

**Odor Control Features** – Look for built‑in carbon filters, sealed lids, or non‑porous surfaces. Many owners pair a good box with clumping litter and a sprinkle of baking soda for extra freshness. For more on keeping the air clean, read our guide on [cat litter odor control](https://happypetproductreviews.com/cat-litter/best-cat-litter-odor-control/).  

**Ease of Cleaning** – A smooth interior and removable tray make scooping less of a chore. Some boxes come with a rake or scoop built into the lid; others rely on you to use a separate tool. Consider how often you’re willing to empty the box—daily scooping keeps odors down, but a larger box can stretch that interval to every few days.  

**Durability** – If you have a curious cat who likes to chew, metal or reinforced plastic will hold up better than thin trays. Heavy‑duty constructions also stand up to the occasional tumble if a cat decides to jump in with gusto.  

By weighing these criteria against your cat’s personality and your cleaning habits, you can narrow down the selection to the perfect match.
