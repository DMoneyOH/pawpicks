**Initial WiFi Setup Can Be Fussy:** While the 5G WiFi compatibility is a great feature once connected, the initial setup process can be a bit finicky for some users. We encountered a few false starts during pairing, requiring us to reset the device and our router's settings a couple of times before achieving a stable connection. This seems to be a common theme in some online reviews as well. While not insurmountable, it could be frustrating for less tech-savvy individuals. Clearer, more detailed troubleshooting steps within the app or manual would greatly enhance the user experience during this crucial first step.

The [PETLIBRO Automatic Cat Feeder 5G WiFi 5L](https://amzn.to/4c4heGp) lacks this feature. While this omission helps keep the price point budget-friendly, it means you'll need a separate pet camera if you want to visually monitor your cat's eating habits or check in on them remotely. For those who prioritize remote visual monitoring and interaction, this could be a drawback.
